Accessibility

Accessibility shouldn't be complicated — or boring. Here's how we do it at Monkeypatch:

  1. Step 1. Free Consultation In-person or in-pixel, we'll walk through your site and goals. No cost, no pressure, just a straightforward chat about where things stand and where to go.
  2. Step 2. Accessibility Audit We check your site against WCAG standards, highlight problem areas, and give you a clear report (written for humans, not robots).
  3. Step 3. Heavy Lifting We fix what needs fixing to bring your site into compliance. The audit cost gets subtracted from the work, because paying twice for the same thing is just silly.
  4. Step 4: Protection continues! We will defend our work and continue to work with you to make sure that your site stays compliant.
